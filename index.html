About Us

AlgaeAfrica Biotech is a pioneering African biotech company specializing in algae-based solutions for water, energy, and agriculture.

Our Mission

To drive Africa’s sustainable bioeconomy by delivering innovative algae-based solutions that create environmental and economic value.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s leading algae technology company, enabling industries and communities to transform waste into renewable energy, clean water, and bio-based products.

Why Choose Us?

Expertise in algae biotechnologies, proven wastewater treatment models, and strategic collaborations across Africa.
